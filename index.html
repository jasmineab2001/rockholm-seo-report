Strategic Overview
Rockholm at the Lighthouse Beach, Kovalam is an NABH-certified luxury Ayurveda resort with strong brand recognition but untapped organic potential. The Phase 1 strategy targets high-intent Ayurveda, wellness, and treatment keywords across English and German markets — building missing pages, recovering dropped rankings, and publishing authoritative blogs that capture both Google and AI-generated search traffic.
German keywords offer the fastest wins with KD scores as low as 7–10, while English recovery pages address 5 urgently dropped pages that previously held top-20 positions. Phase 2 will focus on backlinks and technical SEO after Phase 1 execution is complete.
