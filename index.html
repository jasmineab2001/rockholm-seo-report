Strategic Overview
Rockholm at the Lighthouse Beach, Kovalam is an NABH-certified luxury Ayurveda resort with strong brand recognition but significant untapped organic potential. This Phase 1 plan targets 87 priority keywords across English and German markets — building missing pages, recovering dropped rankings, and publishing authoritative blogs that capture both Google and AI-generated search results. Client keyword research revealed a high-volume Trivandrum resorts cluster (combined ~10,000/mo) and the German pillar "ayurveda kur" (5,400/mo) as the two largest immediate opportunities.

Phase 2 — After Phase 1 Completion
Phase 2 activates once Phase 1 content is fully published and indexed (typically 3–4 months post-publish for new pages). Focus shifts to off-page authority, technical performance, and AI search optimisation using the traffic and ranking data collected during Phase 1.

Backlink Acquisition

Editorial links from travel, wellness, and Ayurveda publications targeting the Kovalam and Kerala pillar pages. Digital PR angles: lighthouse beach location, NABH certification, German Ayurveda tourism.

Technical SEO

Core Web Vitals improvements, hreflang EN/DE implementation, crawl budget optimisation, structured data schema expansion across all pages.

AI Search Optimisation

Structured Q&A content and authoritative entity signals for Google AI Overviews and Perplexity. Phase 1 FAQ schema is the foundation — Phase 2 expands it.

Conversion Optimisation

CRO review of the booking flow, pricing page, and consultation CTAs informed by real Phase 1 traffic patterns and GSC click-through data.
